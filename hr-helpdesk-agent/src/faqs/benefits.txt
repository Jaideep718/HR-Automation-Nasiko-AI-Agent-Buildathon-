Topic: Employee Benefits
Category: Benefits

Q: What benefits does the company offer?
A: The company offers a comprehensive benefits package including:
- Medical, dental, and vision insurance (multiple plan options)
- 401(k) retirement plan with company matching up to 4%
- Life insurance and AD&D coverage
- Short-term and long-term disability insurance
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Flexible Spending Account (FSA) and Health Savings Account (HSA)
- Commuter benefits
- Tuition reimbursement (up to $5,250 per year)
- Gym membership discount

Q: When can I enroll in benefits?
A: Benefits enrollment is available during:
- New hire enrollment window: within 30 days of your start date
- Annual open enrollment: held each November for the following calendar year
- Qualifying life events (marriage, divorce, birth/adoption of child, loss of other coverage) within 30 days of the event

Q: How do I change my benefits elections?
A: Outside of open enrollment, you can only change benefits due to a qualifying life event. Submit your change request in the HR Portal under "Benefits" > "Change Request" within 30 days of the qualifying event.

Q: What is the 401(k) match policy?
A: The company matches 50% of your contributions up to 8% of your salary, meaning a maximum match of 4%. You are fully vested in company contributions after 3 years of service.

Q: How do I contact the benefits administrator?
A: Contact the Benefits Team at benefits@company.com or call (555) 123-4569. For insurance carrier questions, refer to your benefits guide for carrier contact information.

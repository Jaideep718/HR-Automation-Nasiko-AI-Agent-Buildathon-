Topic: Onboarding and New Employee Information
Category: Onboarding

Q: What should I do on my first day?
A: On your first day:
1. Report to the reception desk in Building A – security will have your visitor badge ready
2. You will meet your HR onboarding contact for a welcome session
3. Your manager will provide a team introduction and first-week schedule
4. IT will set up your laptop and accounts (allow up to 2 hours)
5. You will complete required compliance training modules online

Q: How long does onboarding take?
A: The formal onboarding process spans your first 90 days (30-60-90 day plan). You will have check-ins with HR and your manager to review your progress and address any questions.

Q: What documents do I need to bring on my first day?
A: Bring two forms of identification for I-9 verification (e.g., passport, driver's license + Social Security card). A full list of acceptable documents is in your offer letter package.

Q: When will I receive my employee ID and badge?
A: Your employee ID is assigned before your start date and sent via email. Your physical access badge will be ready for pickup at Security on your first day.

Q: Who is my onboarding contact in HR?
A: You will be assigned a dedicated HR onboarding specialist before your start date. Their contact information will be in your pre-boarding email. You can also reach general HR onboarding support at onboarding@company.com.

Q: When does my benefits enrollment start?
A: Your benefits are effective on your first day of employment. You must complete your enrollment elections within 30 days of your start date through the HR Portal.

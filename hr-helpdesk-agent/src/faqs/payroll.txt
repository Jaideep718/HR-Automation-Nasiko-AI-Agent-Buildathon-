Topic: Payroll and Salary
Category: Finance / Compensation

Q: When is payday?
A: Payroll is processed bi-weekly on Fridays. Direct deposits arrive in your bank account by 9 AM on payday. Paper checks, if requested, are distributed by end of business on the same Friday.

Q: How do I view my pay stub?
A: Pay stubs are available in the HR Portal under "Payroll" > "Pay Stubs". You can view and download your last 24 months of pay stubs at any time.

Q: What do I do if I notice an error in my pay?
A: Immediately contact Payroll at payroll@company.com or call (555) 123-4568. Provide your employee ID, pay period in question, and a description of the discrepancy. Corrections are typically processed in the next pay cycle.

Q: How do I update my direct deposit information?
A: Log into the HR Portal, navigate to "Payroll" > "Direct Deposit", and update your bank account details. Changes must be submitted at least 5 business days before the next pay date to take effect.

Q: Are overtime payments included in the bi-weekly paycheck?
A: Yes, approved overtime hours worked within the pay period are included in the same bi-weekly paycheck. Overtime must be pre-approved by your manager.

Q: Who do I contact about my W-2 or tax forms?
A: W-2 forms are mailed and available digitally in the HR Portal by January 31st each year. For questions, contact payroll@company.com.

Topic: HR Contact Information and Support
Category: HR Support

Q: How do I contact the HR team?
A: HR Team Contact Information:
- Email: hr@company.com
- Phone: (555) 123-4567
- Office Location: Building A, 2nd Floor, Room 210
- Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM (Eastern Time)

Q: What issues does HR handle?
A: The HR team handles a wide range of topics, including:
- Employee onboarding and offboarding
- Benefits enrollment and questions
- Leave of absence and FMLA requests
- Workplace complaints and investigations
- Performance management and reviews
- Compensation and salary questions
- Company policy clarifications
- Training and development programs

Q: Is there an HR emergency contact?
A: For urgent or after-hours HR matters, email hr-urgent@company.com. For workplace safety emergencies, call building security at (555) 123-9999 or dial 911 for life-threatening emergencies.

Q: How do I submit a confidential HR concern?
A: You can submit a confidential concern through the Ethics Hotline at https://ethics.company.com or call (800) 555-0100. Reports can be submitted anonymously.

Q: How long does it take for HR to respond?
A: Standard HR inquiries are answered within 2 business days. Escalated tickets are prioritized by urgency: urgent issues within 4 hours, high priority within 1 business day, and medium/low priority within 2-3 business days.

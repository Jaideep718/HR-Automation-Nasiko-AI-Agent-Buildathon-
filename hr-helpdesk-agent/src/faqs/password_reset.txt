Topic: Password Reset and Login Issues
Category: IT Support / Account Access

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, visit the IT Self-Service Portal at https://itportal.company.com. Click "Forgot Password" and follow the on-screen instructions. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

Q: Who do I contact for login problems?
A: Contact the IT Help Desk at helpdesk@company.com or call (555) 987-6543. The IT Help Desk is available Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM.

Q: What are the password requirements?
A: Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and at least one special character. Passwords expire every 90 days.

Q: I am locked out of my account. What should I do?
A: After 5 failed login attempts your account will be locked. You can unlock it via the IT Self-Service Portal or contact the IT Help Desk at helpdesk@company.com.

Q: Can I use my work login for personal devices?
A: Company credentials should only be used on approved corporate devices or through the company VPN. Do not store your credentials in personal browsers.
